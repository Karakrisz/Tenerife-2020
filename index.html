The Windsor feeling

Imagine waking up late, having your coffee with a toast on the balcony while you gaze at the Ocean. You wonder whether you would like to stay at home and enjoy the heated pool or go for some adventures at "El Teide". The evening offers some excitements...beach, bars and shops which are only 15 min away on foot. Are you coming with family? Aqualand and Siam Park are next door! If you get hungry, restaurants and supermarkets are nearby! At the end of the day, you will fall asleep on Netflix…

The area…

The area is very clean and friendly. The building has a well-managed garden and yard with original palms and cactuses, typical on this vulcanic island. Feel free to take an evening-walk among the luxury apartments or to the hill where you can see San Eugenio Bajo at night. There is a well equipped gym (RED GYM) I highly recommend, if there is still extra energy left to let go.:) For bigger food shoppings Mercadona -similar to Lidl, Aldi, Spar, Tesco, Auchan, Carrefour, or Cora - you need to go to ( 1 km, 15 min walk or 2 min by car).

To get around..

South Tenerife Airport is about 20 mins drive on TF-1 Mororway (free). Exit 76. Taxi costs about 30 EUR. If you prefer a private airport pick-up ( and I am available), message me prior to your arrival.The bus stop is to 20 meters from the apartment but taxis are also available. Bus 424 takes you to quite many places: Aqualand, Siam, Magma (police station), Zentral Center, Los Christianos. Comes in every 20-30 mins from 7:51-21:35. Bus stop number at Windsor Park: 8240.
